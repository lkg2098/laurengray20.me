My First Character Animation

This is a video created for the final submission of my animation class on the fundamentals of animation. I love musical theater and had been listening to a lot of music from the Dear Evan Hansen soundtrack at the time. I also knew that I wanted my final project to include lipsync, as I really enjoy the technical nature of the lipsyncing process along with the artistic choices of character posing and facial expression.
